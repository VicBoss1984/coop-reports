Information about the Employer

Founded in 2022 by University of Guelph alumni Daniel Kolodziejczak and Jacob Claessens, Oriole A.I. stands at the forefront of a unique and philosophical approach to artificial intelligence. Headquartered in Guelph, Ontario, this innovative company harnesses the talents of a diverse team of engineers and marketers to pioneer a path distinct from the prevailing AI research trends.

Unlike the majority of AI research groups focused on Advanced General Intelligence (AGI) in 2023, Oriole A.I. diverges with its belief in the development of embodied artificial intelligence. This philosophical stance stems from a fundamental belief in the spiritual dimension of intelligence, a trait they assert cannot be fully replicated or simulated through mechanical processes alone. This perspective challenges the conventional notion that the human brain's functions are purely mechanical and can be reverse-engineered for computer simulation.

At the heart of Oriole's philosophy is the conviction that the ultimate engineering feat is the recreation of man, even in synthetic form. This subtle but profound difference in vision significantly influences the company's product development strategies. Oriole A.I. emphasizes the importance of considering the hardware, alongside the software, of a system. This approach leads to a holistic view of technology development, focusing on the oneness of the system and its alignment with human needs, whether for augmentation or autonomy.

Oriole A.I.'s commitment to this philosophy is evident in its design principles. The company recognizes that for an AI system to accomplish tasks efficiently, it needs a general working knowledge. Oriole A.I. has identified the GPT system as the optimal solution for this requirement, owing to its remarkable alignment and effectiveness, a concept also espoused by AI pioneer Yann LeCun.

In summary, Oriole A.I. is not just a company but a visionary entity. It is a place where the lines between engineering, philosophy, and spirituality blur to create AI solutions that are not just technologically advanced but also aligned with a deeper understanding of human essence and needs. As the company moves forward, it continues to redefine the boundaries of AI, making significant contributions to both the field and to the communities it serves.

Transitioning to the second half of my term, my focus shifted exclusively to mobile application development. Here, I engaged in developing the 'Oriole A.I.' Android application, where I not only honed my skills in Android app development but also learned and applied Kotlin extensively. Further details about the specific projects and contributions I made during my tenure at Oriole A.I. will be discussed later in this report.

Job Description

As a mobile developer at Oriole AI Inc., my role was multifaceted and dynamic, encompassing a range of responsibilities that required both technical expertise and creative thinking. Here's an expanded overview of my key responsibilities:

  • New Feature Development and Bug Fixing: Tasked with both creating innovative features for the mobile apps and addressing and resolving any functional or performance issues, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Leveraging Cloud Services: Utilized cloud services to enhance app functionality, improve scalability, and ensure reliable data management and processing.
  • Artificial Intelligence Integration: Incorporated AI technology to elevate the application experience, introducing intelligent features that catered to user needs and preferences.
  • Task Management and Prioritization: Effectively managed and prioritized development tasks, balancing immediate needs with long-term project goals.
  • Team Coordination and Communication: Communicated the level of effort required for development tasks and delegated tasks appropriately, ensuring efficient teamwork and project progression.
  • Third-party Service Integration: Integrated various third-party services and systems into the mobile app, enhancing its capabilities and user options.
  • Research and Innovation: Conducted research into new technologies and libraries that could be leveraged to create novel features or product offerings, keeping our app at the forefront of innovation.
  • Enhancing User Experience for Subscription Growth: Focused on improving user experience to drive product subscriptions, understanding that a satisfied user is more likely to invest in our product.
  • Strategic Feature Development: Considered the business impact and strategic positioning of new features, ensuring that each development was aligned with the company's vision and goals.
  • Value Communication: Developed strategies to communicate the value of new developments to users, tailoring our message for various use cases to maximize engagement and understanding.
  • Build Management and Deployment: Managed builds and oversaw deployment processes, ensuring that updates and new features were smoothly integrated and released.
  • Documentation: Maintained comprehensive documentation of development processes, providing a clear guide for future development efforts and team members.
  • Marketing Support: Created promotional videos to showcase the product, contributing to marketing efforts and user engagement.
  • Hardware Integration: Worked on interfacing with hardware that integrates with the application, supporting new device development and expanding the app's ecosystem.

Collaboration was the cornerstone of my experience at Oriole AI Inc. The opportunity to bridge the gap between technical and non-technical domains, particularly in the context of mobile development, was both a challenge and a rewarding aspect of my role.

The skills required for this job were diverse, ranging from technical proficiencies in cloud services and AI integration to soft skills like team coordination and effective communication. While my academic background at the University of Guelph provided a solid foundation in these areas, the hands-on experience gained during this work-term was invaluable. It not only reinforced my existing skills but also introduced me to new technologies and approaches, emphasizing the importance of adaptability and continuous learning in the fast-evolving field of mobile development.
